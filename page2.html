Environment

Spongebob takes place in Bikini Bottom, a small town under the sea. The geography consists of things such as mountains, caves, and reefs. Some notable locations include Goo Lagoon, Palm Bay, Seashell Park, and many others. This environment has an abundance of fish, crabs, and other sea animals, with the exception of the infamous Spongebob Squarepants and Sandy Cheeks the squirrel.
